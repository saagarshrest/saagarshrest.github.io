üß† Overview

Officiti is a B2B concept platform designed to optimize underutilized office spaces by allowing companies to rent out redundant desks, workstations, or entire units to smaller businesses and freelancers seeking flexible workspaces.

It bridges the gap between space availability and the rising demand for flexible, accessible working environments, especially in urban centers where commercial rents are high and quality co-working spaces are limited.
